Learning Outcome: Users will learn how to declutter their own home, mind and digital space through various clickable items's that give practical action guides and basic instructions on how to's. The website provides value to users by implementing emotional design into the website. For example, users feel good when the cluttered items automatically declutter and dissapear from the room once a user clicks and views the information. After using the interactive website, users will feel prompted to clean their own space.
